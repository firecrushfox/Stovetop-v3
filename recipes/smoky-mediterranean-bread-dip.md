# Smoky Mediterranean Bread Dip

> Source: 

## Description
POV: You pull up with this Smoky Mediterranean Dipping Oil and now you’re forever in charge of appetizers 🤤🍅🫒🍋

It’s garlicky, lemony, a little smoky… and gone in minutes. Like, people hovering over the bread type of gone!

Save this and send it to your dip partner 🫠

## Ingredients
- 1 cup Warm Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- 2 tbsp Aged Balsamic Vinegar
- 1/4 cup Chopped Sun Dried Tomatoes
- 1/4 cup Chopped Castelvetrano Pitted Olives
- 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
- 1/4 cup Chopped Italian Parsley
- 2 tbsp Basil Chiffonade
- Zest 1 Lemon
- 1 tsp Spanish Smoked Paprika
- 2 tsp Red Pepper Flake
- Pinch of Flaky Salt & Pepper
- Fresh Grated Parm

## Instructions
1. Mix all this goodness together and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes to let the flavors wake up! 🫠
2. Serve with some warm ciabatta bread and probably double the recipe because this one goes so fast!🫠

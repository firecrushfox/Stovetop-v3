# Veggie Quiche

> Source: https://onebalancedlife.com/veggie-quiche/

## Description
This Veggie Quiche is perfect to add to your brunch or meal prep for the week!

## Ingredients
- Frozen Premade Pie Crust, thawed and refrigerated
- ½ Cup Cooked Ground Turkey Breakfast Sausage or Cooked Chopped Bacon (optional)
- 2 Tablespoons Oil (olive, canola, or avocado oil)
- ½ Yellow or White Onion, diced
- 1 Red Bell Pepper, small diced
- 4 oz. Baby Bella Mushrooms, sliced
- 3 Cups Fresh Baby Spinach, chopped ***(or ½ cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained)***
- 2 Teaspoons Minced Garlic
- 7 Large Eggs
- ¾ Cup Cottage Cheese
- ½ Cup Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
- ¼ Cup Crumbled Feta Cheese
- ½ Teaspoon Salt
- ½ Teaspoon Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
2. Gently shape your refrigerated pie crust into a pie pan, shape the edges, and poke a few holes in it with a fork. Bake for 5 - 6 minutes, or until slightly puffed up and cooked through. Set aside to cool.
3. Place a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once heated, add the oil and allow to heat slightly before adding the diced onions and bell peppers. Sauté for 2 - 3 minutes to soften slightly. Add the mushrooms and a pinch of salt and cook for another 2 - 3 minutes. Add the spinach and garlic and cook for another 1 - 2 minutes or until the spinach has wilted fully and the garlic is aromatic. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool.
4. If using sausage, brown in a heated non-stick skillet and set aside.
5. To a large bowl add the eggs, cottage cheese, monterey jack cheese, feta cheese, salt, and black pepper. Whisk thoroughly to combine.
6. Add the cooled vegetable mixture from the skillet and mix thoroughly to combine. Add in sausage if using too.
7. Pour the quiche filling into the partially baked pie crust. Put dish on a baking pan and place in the oven.
8. Bake for between an hour and an hour and 10 minutes. After an hour, check the center of the quiche with a toothpick to make sure it is fully cooked and not runny/liquid in the center.
9. After baking, allow the quiche to cool for 20+ minutes before serving.
10. Enjoy!


## Notes
- You may need to add additional time if center of quiche is liquidy.
- *I recommend placing your pie pan on a baking sheet pan.
- *Feel free to cover the edges of the pie with aluminum foil if you’re concerned about the crust burning or getting too dark. (my crust was a nutty golden brown after baking without an aluminum foil cover)
